FaQs

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please chat to our friendly team or write us an email at info@alimun.com

  • How can your classes be so affordable compared to private tutors?

    We use a high-leverage model, not 1:1 tutoring. One great teacher serves many learners simultaneously, allowing us to offer daily live access without the high hourly cost.

  • Does a group class really improve my speaking fluency?

    Yes, through structured interaction. Our methodology focuses on using small breakout rooms where every student gets focused speaking time and real-time guidance.

  • What if I have an unpredictable schedule?

    Our platform is built for flexibility. We offer unlimited daily drop-in sessions across multiple time zones. You join when it fits your schedule—no fixed commitment is needed.

  • Who are the teachers?

    Our teachers go through a two-stage vetting process: application review (CV, credentials, video intro) followed by a two-session trial with a test group. Teachers are assigned to tiers based on experience — only our best run Intensive and Private cohorts. We promise real human feedback and guidance—not a bot.

  • What do I get with the subscription?

    You get unlimited daily access to all live, structured conversation and grammar sessions at your proficiency level. You can drop in any time, every day.

  • How do I sign up for membership?

    We are launching our founding cohort now — 200 spots at a permanently discounted price for early members. Join at alimun.com/signup to claim yours before they fill. To ensure you're one of the first to join, simply enter your email on our homepage to Join the Waitlist. You will receive an exclusive invitation to enroll the moment we officially launch our first tiers.
