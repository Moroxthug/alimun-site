Our story

Built by language teachers who got tired of watching great students stall out in expensive, infrequent 1:1 lessons. Alimun pairs daily live practice with AI feedback so progress happens between classes too, not just during them.

A Standard for Real Fluency

Built since day one around one idea: a great teacher can guide many students at once if the AI handles the repetitive grading. That belief shapes every tier, every class, and every feature we ship.
